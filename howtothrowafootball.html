How Do You Throw the Ball?

To learn how to throw the ball, watch the video below.

You can also read these instructions if the video was too fast for you!

  1. First, hold the ball with your fingers in between the grooves of the white lacings.
  2. Second, hold the ball so that your thumb is touching your ear.
  3. Finally, throw the ball with your fingers sliding of the side, making a nice spiral motion with the ball.

How to Throw a Football!

What is Football?

Football is a sport played by two teams of eleven players on a rectangular field with goalposts at each end. Each team tries to get more points than their opponents. In order to do that, they must score a "touchdown." A touchdown is when at least one player from a team reaches the other side of the field with the ball in their hands. The game keeps playing until the four quarters have been completed. Then, the points are tallied up, and the winner is announced! To learn more, you can click on this link! -> Wikipedia


How Do You Throw the Ball?

To learn how to throw the ball, watch the video below.

You can also read these instructions if the video was too fast for you!

  1. First, hold the ball with your fingers in between the grooves of the white lacings.
  2. Second, hold the ball so that your thumb is touching your ear.
  3. Finally, throw the ball with your fingers sliding of the side, making a nice spiral motion with the ball.
